About the Humans

Carrots Cannoli was born out of love, laughter, and a ginger cat named Carrots. It started as a daydream shared by two women in a tiny kitchen — one filled with the smell of espresso, the warmth of each other's company, and a cat who always found the sunniest spot on the floor.

We believe desserts are more than just sweet treats. They're memories in the making, comfort in a bite, and love stories in sugar and cream. Our goal is to create a space that feels like a hug from an old friend.

We hope that Carrots Cannoli brings a little magic into your day, a reminder to savor the small moments, and maybe even the inspiration to follow your own sweet little dream.

Our Vision

We dare for more than cannoli. We dream of creating a cozy world where imagination, identity, and indulgence collide. Whether you're stopping by for a bite of story, a giggle over a cat's unnecesarily dramatic backstory, or to imagine your perfect cannoli creation - we want you to feel at home.

In this bakery of dreams, you're not just a visitor. You're part of the tale. Come for the whimsy, stay for the sweetness, and share in something heartfelt.
