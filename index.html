Meet Carrots

Carrots is our round, ginger boy with a big heart and a love for snuggles. He’s been the muse behind this whimsical little dream. With his big eyes, gentle purr, and slightly dramatic energy, Carrots Cannoli was born as a tribute to joy, love, and dessert.

The Lore

Long ago, in a sunlit kitchen nestled between mountains and dreams, there lived a round ginger cat named Carrots Cannoli. He spent his days watching the world from the window, waiting for his beloved — Sir Parsnip — a noble soul off on a grand adventure.

Born of longing, love, and the scent of powdered sugar, Carrots Cannoli the shop became a sweet tribute to those who wait, to those who believe, and to the power of dessert to bring hearts home.
